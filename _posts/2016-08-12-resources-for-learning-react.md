Nitin Punjabi over at [patternhatch](http://patternhatch.com) has written some 
really great tutorials for getting started in the ReactJS ecosystem. 
In [part 1](http://patternhatch.com/2016/07/06/a-primer-on-the-react-ecosystem-part-1-of-3/) 
you create the basic scaffolding for a ReactJS app, while [part 2](http://patternhatch.com/2016/08/02/a-primer-on-the-react-ecosystem-part-2-of-3/) guides you through the 
creation of a Spotify client. Writing good tutorials is hard work, so a big 'thank you' to him for writing these!
